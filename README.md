## About Me

Research Scientist and Technical Expert at Huawei Technologies CO., Ltd, Wireless Network Research Department. I received my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Tsinghua University (EE) and was a visiting scholar at MIT (EECS).

**Research Interests:**

- 5G-A and 6G wireless communications (5G-A/6G)
- Machine learning for wireless communications (AI4NET)
- Electromagnetic information theory (EIT)
- Visible light communications (VLC)
